What is Map 501?
In short: this is a starting place.
Map 501 is meant for people who are in the nonprofit sector and have realized that they need help getting started adding mapping tools into their organization.

This site is not meant to teach you everything you need to know, but rather get you pointed in the right direction and give you a path to success.
